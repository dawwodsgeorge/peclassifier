# Privacy Policy for PEClassifier

**Last updated:** July 6, 2026

## Overview

PEClassifier is a clinical reference tool designed to help healthcare professionals apply the 2026 AHA/ACC/ACCP/ACEP/CHEST acute pulmonary embolism classification system. This policy explains how the app handles data.

## Data Collection

PEClassifier does **not** collect, transmit, or store any personally identifiable patient information.

The app includes an optional feature that allows users to save a structured, non-identifying clinical history summary (e.g., classification category, relevant clinical parameters used to arrive at that classification). This information:

- Is stored **locally on your device only**
- Is never transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party
- Does not include patient names, dates of birth, medical record numbers, or any other identifying information
- Can be deleted at any time by the user within the app or by deleting the app

## Analytics and Third-Party Services

PEClassifier does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data from your use of the app is shared with or sold to any third party.

## Data Storage and Security

Any data saved within the app resides in the app's local storage on your device, protected by your device's built-in security features (e.g., passcode, Face ID, Touch ID). Uninstalling the app permanently removes this data.

## Children's Privacy

PEClassifier is intended for use by licensed healthcare professionals, medical students, and clinical trainees. It is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Intended Use and Disclaimer

PEClassifier is a clinical reference and educational tool. It is **not a diagnostic device** and is not intended to replace clinical judgment, established diagnostic protocols, or consultation with qualified healthcare providers. The app displays a disclaimer to this effect on a recurring basis.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.
